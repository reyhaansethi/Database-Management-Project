Query 4

Definition of a sprint race from Motorsport Magazine: "Sprint races run to 100km (62 miles). Lap count varies depending on the circuit. For the Sao Paulo GP, the sprint race is set for 24 laps, which is just over a third of the 71-lap grand prix distance. There is also a time limit: races must be completed within 60 minutes, so if safety cars and stoppages take the action beyond an hour, it will be ended early." https://www.motorsportmagazine.com/articles/single-seaters/f1/f1-sprint-races-what-are-they-and-how-will-they-work/
